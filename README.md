# Canadian-Peregine-Falcon-Population-Data
An analysis of the Canadian Population of Peregine Falcons and how they trend from 1992 all the way towards 2020. 
The sharp and sudden increase of the species is most likely due to the conservation efforts in Ontario and British Columbia. Local pesticides that decimated the local population in the 1950's were rolled back and introduction of over 100 new breeding pairs in the 2010's, leads to the average 5% increase in the population growth rate over the 2010's.
